About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher with a strong focus on mathematics and its diverse applications in fields such as theoretical computer science, machine learning, and signal processing. My primary academic interest lies in uncovering the mathematical structures that underpin phenomena often perceived as non-mathematical. I am particularly motivated by the systematic study of how complex systems function and take great interest in learning and applying advanced mathematical tools to analyze these systems. Currently, I am engaged in research projects on fair machine learning theory, game theory, and facility location.
