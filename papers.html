Accepted Papers

  • Avyukta Manjunatha Vummintala, Shantanu Das, Sujit Gujar. FROC: Building Fair ROC from a Trained Classifier. (Full paper) Accepted at AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI 2025)
    • Fairness in Probabilistic Classification: Proposed a novel fairness concept, ε-Equalized ROC, ensuring parity in false positive and true positive rates across protected groups.
    • Theoretical Insights: Conducted rigorous analysis on the trade-offs of achieving ε-Equalized ROC, introducing a threshold query model for theoretical underpinning.
    • Algorithmic Contribution: Designed FROC, a linear-time algorithm with optimal guarantees, validated across diverse datasets and classifiers.
  • Avyukta Manjunatha Vummintala, Shivam Gupta, Shweta Jain, Sujit Gujar. FLIGHT: Facility Location Integrating Generalized, Holistic Theory of Welfare. (Full paper) Accepted at International Conference on Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems (AAMAS 2025)
    • Novel Framework: Generalized the Facility Location Problem (FLP) to incorporate diverse welfare objectives, including asymmetric considerations.
    • Theoretical Foundations: Provided structural properties and approximation guarantees for FLP solutions.
    • Real-World Implications: Derived concentration bounds under distributional assumptions, offering practical guidance for large-scale implementations.
