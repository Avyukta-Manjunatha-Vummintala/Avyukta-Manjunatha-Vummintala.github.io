Mathematical Puzzles

Challenge your mind with these thought-provoking puzzles:

  • Prime Paths: Find the shortest path between two prime numbers by changing one digit at a time, ensuring all intermediate numbers are also prime.
  • Magic Squares: Arrange numbers in a square grid so that every row, column, and diagonal sums to the same value.
  • The Towers of Hanoi: Solve the classic problem of moving disks between three pegs under specific constraints.

Logic Puzzles

  • Einstein's Riddle: A challenging logic puzzle involving a series of clues to determine who owns the fish.
  • Knights and Knaves: Solve puzzles involving inhabitants who always tell the truth or always lie.
  • River Crossing: Help a group of people cross a river with various constraints on who can travel together.
